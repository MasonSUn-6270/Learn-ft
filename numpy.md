## 转置索引

### np.newaxis

```python
np.linspace(0,10,5)[np.newaxis,np.newaxis]
array([[[ 0. ,  2.5,  5. ,  7.5, 10. ]]])
np.linspace(0,10,5)[np.newaxis]
array([[ 0. ,  2.5,  5. ,  7.5, 10. ]])
np.linspace(0,10,5)[:,np.newaxis]
array([[ 0. ],
       [ 2.5],
       [ 5. ],
       [ 7.5],
       [10. ]])
```
